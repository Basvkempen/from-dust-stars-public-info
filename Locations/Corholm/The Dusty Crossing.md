# The Dusty Crossing

*Tavern. The social heart of Corholm.*

Two stories, timber and plaster, a fire that's never gone out. Common room downstairs, four guest rooms above, a back room for private conversation.

**Who's here**
- [Marta Vale](../../NPCs/Marta%20Vale.html) — runs the place
- [Old Bren](../../NPCs/Old%20Bren.html) — corner table most evenings
- [Edrik Mourn](../../NPCs/Edrik%20Mourn.html) — regular stop on his route
