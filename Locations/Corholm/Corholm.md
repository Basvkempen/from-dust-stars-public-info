# Corholm

*Home. In [The Dustlands](../../Lore/The%20Dustlands.html).*

A village of roughly 500 souls, two days' walk west of **Duskmoor** along the trade road that winds toward **Grimbarrow**. Corholm exists because of where it sits — a crossroads where the main trade road meets a smaller path leading south to the old quarry and the scattered homesteads beyond.

It's not much to look at. Nobody important lives here. No noble has visited in living memory. But there's honest work, and the rhythm of the place is yours.

## Points of Interest

- [The Dusty Crossing](The%20Dusty%20Crossing.html) — the tavern. Everyone ends up here eventually.
- [Market Square](Market%20Square.html) — the heart of things. A worn stone well, trade stalls every Tensday.
- [Veln's General Goods](Veln's%20General%20Goods.html) — dry goods, tools, and the local True Hand outpost.
- The Smithy — the only blacksmith for a day's travel. *(more to come)*
- The Sunshade Chapel — a small stone temple serving Solarian, Verdania, and Drakmoth under one roof. *(more to come)*
