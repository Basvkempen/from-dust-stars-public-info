# Market Square

*Open square. The centre of village life.*

A worn stone well at the middle, with a faded ribbon tied around it — nobody remembers why. Trade stalls set up every Tensday.

**Who's here**
- [Edrik Mourn](../../NPCs/Edrik%20Mourn.md) — sets up a stall on Tensday
