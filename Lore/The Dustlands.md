# The Dustlands

*Eastern Eldoria. Part of [Eldoria](Eldoria.html).*

- Flat plains and badlands, rising into the Grayvein foothills.
- **Duskmoor** — the major city. Fog-wrapped, three rivers meet. Seat of the Council of Veils.
- **Grimbarrow** — mining town in the mountain foothills.
- **Corholm** — your home village.
- The **Iron Serpent** — the great train line connecting Dustlands settlements.
- The **True Hand Trade Company** — runs the trade routes, funds the mines, owns the Iron Serpent. Symbol: an open palm grasping a coin.
