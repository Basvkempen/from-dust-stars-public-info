# Marta Vale

*Halfling. Baker. Runs [The Dusty Crossing](../Locations/Corholm/The%20Dusty%20Crossing.html)'s kitchen and her own bakery on the western road.*

Up before dawn most days. Knows everyone's usual order. Gives a bit more than the price asks.
